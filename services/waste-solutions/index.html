Expert Solid Waste Management in Ghana

Jekora Ventures provides premier solid waste management services for residents, businesses, and organizations throughout the Greater Accra Region. As the only company accredited under the Fee and Performance-Based Solid Waste Collection Franchise Agreement for the Korle Klotey, Adentan, and Weija Gbawe Municipal areas, we are your trusted partner in promoting environmental sanitation and public health.

Our proficient refuse collection crews, equipped with modern compaction trucks, specialized "Borla Taxis," and skip loaders, ensure timely and high-quality service. All collected waste is safely transported to designated transfer stations or final disposal sites, adhering to all local regulations. Our customer-focused team is always available to address your unique needs and resolve any operational challenges swiftly.
