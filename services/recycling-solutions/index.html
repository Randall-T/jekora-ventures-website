Ghana's Leader in Source Segregation & Recycling

Jekora Ventures is proud to be the first and only Ghanaian waste management company to offer a comprehensive, incentive-based recycling program alongside solid waste source segregation. Our pioneering program rewards commercial and industrial clients with discounts on their monthly bills for efficiently segregating their waste into recyclable streams.

With over 120 businesses participating, we are making the principle of recycling a practical and beneficial reality in Accra. While this service is currently focused on our commercial clients in the Osu Klottey and Adentan municipalities, residents are welcome to drop off sorted recyclables at our stations in Asylum Down, Odawna, or Jamestown.

Organic Waste Recycling

We collect and process biodegradable materials like food, market, and garden waste using designated green 240-litre bins. This organic waste is transformed into our high-quality JV CompSoil and Fortifer compost products, enriching Ghana's soil.

Plastic Waste Recycling

Our program targets plastic waste such as water sachets and bottles, collected in designated blue 240-litre bins. Through our trusted partners, this plastic is processed into new domestic products, toys, and other useful items.

Paper Waste Recycling

We provide dedicated collection services for paper and cardboard waste from offices and institutions. This material is then recycled into new paper products, reducing deforestation and conserving natural resources.

Textile Waste Recycling

Our textile recycling service collects discarded cloth, materials, and sewn clothes in designated black 240-litre bins. Our partners repurpose these textiles into new bags, accessories, and other innovative products.
